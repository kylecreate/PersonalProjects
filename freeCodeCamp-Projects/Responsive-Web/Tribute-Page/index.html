Lee Sang-hyeok (aka Faker)

The true G.O.A.T of professional League of Legends

Facts about Lee Sang-hyeok's career

  • As of November 2024, he's won 5 League of Legends World Championships making him the only one to do so.
  • He's won two mid-season invitationals (2016 and 2017).
  • During his time in the LCK (League of Legends Champions Korea), he's won 9 titles since starting professional play in 2013.
  • He's been with the same team of SK T1 since 2013 (12 years) and has since became part-owner of the team since 2020.
  • His debut stream on Twitch in early 2017, he broke the record of having 245,000 concurrent viewers watching him.
  • He was the first player in LCK history to reach 2,500 kills in 2022 as his 10th professional year of playing.
  • When and if he retires from competitive gaming, he wants to study. However, he's unsure of what he wants to study.
